Overview

ngUI is a small project that gave me the opportunity to explore further Angular's routing capabilities, Directives and Lifecycle Hooks to make a component as reusable as possible.


The goal was to experiment with different Angular's features combined with Sass to sharpen my Frontend skills while designing something colorful and fun in Figma.
