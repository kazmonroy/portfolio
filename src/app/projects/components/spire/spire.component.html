Opal is a project management tool with CRUD features such as creating new boards, adding/editing tasks and show real-time updates.


It's a MEAN Full Stack app with Socket.io to enhance live updates among clients. The Design System is inspired by the current Dark Mode design trend where Material Design's principles were applied to maximize usability and accessibility.


This project allowed me to scratch the surface of Full Stack development and experiment with new technologies to create something that others will be pleased to use.
